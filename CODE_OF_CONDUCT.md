# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

1. Be respectful and constructive.
2. Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
3. Focus feedback on code and ideas, not people.
4. Accept responsibility and apologize when needed.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
2. Trolling, insults, or deliberately inflammatory comments.
3. Publishing private information without consent.
4. Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation for violations.

## Reporting

Report incidents to `conduct@your-org.example`.
